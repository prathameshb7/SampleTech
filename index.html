Business Process Reengineering (BPR)

BPR is about creating a new system or redesigning a workflow within an organization for betterment of the business. This involves changes in two main areas – first being the usage of modern technologies, and second being building up functional teams.
If the answer to any of the questions below is “YES”, then the organization needs to implement BPR.

  1. Are we providing services at higher costs than our industry?
  2. Is there a newer way of work that we have not brought into our processes?
  3. Do our processes seem overly complex?
  4. Are too many people doing too many similar things?

Techforaz Consultants help in implementing BPR to improve quality of services and reduce costs by

  1. Measuring the organizations goal and objectives
  2. Identify the processes
  3. Incorporating IT solutions
